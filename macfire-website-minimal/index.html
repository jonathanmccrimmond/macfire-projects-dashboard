Our services

MacFire Ltd provides a complete range of fire protection services for commercial and industrial premises across Scotland. All work carried out to current British Standards and fire safety legislation.

Fire Alarm Systems

Supply, installation and maintenance of high-quality fire alarm systems for commercial and industrial sites. Fully compliant with current British Standards.

Fire Extinguishers

All extinguisher types supplied, installed and serviced by BAFE-registered technicians in accordance with BS 5306 Part 3. Refills and repairs where possible.

Fire Extinguisher Training

On-site training by qualified instructors so your staff have the knowledge and confidence to act safely in an emergency. Compliant with current fire safety legislation.

Dry Risers & Fire Hydrants

Statutory six-monthly testing of dry risers and fire hydrants by skilled engineers, keeping responsible owners and operators compliant with British Standards.

Fire Risk Assessments

A thorough inspection of your premises with a detailed report evaluating existing precautions and any remedial action required. Free, no-obligation initial consultation.

Fire Safety Signage

Authorised distributor of Jalite AAA photoluminescent signage. We advise on, supply and install the correct signs for your premises and sector.

About MacFire Ltd

MacFire Ltd is an independent fire protection company based in Barrhead, East Renfrewshire, serving businesses across Scotland since 2006. We bring over 75 years of combined industry experience to every contract.

As BAFE SP101-registered contractors and ISO 9001:2015 certified, we hold ourselves to the highest professional standards. We are also Safe Contractor approved and operate in full compliance with BS 5306 Part 3 and all relevant fire safety legislation.

Unlike national chains, we offer the responsiveness, accountability and personal service of an independent specialist, with the technical capability to service multi-site national contracts.
