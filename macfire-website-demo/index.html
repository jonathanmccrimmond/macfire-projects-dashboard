What we do

Six services. One trusted partner.

Everything you need to keep your premises compliant with current fire safety legislation and British Standards, under one roof, from one accountable team.

Fire Alarm Systems

Supply, installation and maintenance of high-quality fire alarm systems for commercial and industrial sites. Fully compliant with current British Standards.

Fire Extinguishers

All extinguisher types supplied, installed and serviced by BAFE-registered technicians in accordance with BS5306 Part 3. Refills and repairs where possible, no wasteful replacements.

Fire Extinguisher Training

On-site training delivered by qualified instructors so your staff have the knowledge and confidence to act in an emergency. Compliant with all relevant fire safety legislation.

Dry Risers & Fire Hydrants

Statutory six-monthly testing of dry risers and fire hydrants by skilled engineers, keeping responsible owners and operators compliant with British Standards.

Fire Risk Assessments

A thorough inspection of your premises and a detailed report evaluating existing precautions and any remedial action required. Free, no-obligation initial consultation.

Fire Safety Signage

Authorised distributor of Jalite AAA photoluminescent signage. We advise, supply and install the correct signs for your premises and sector.
