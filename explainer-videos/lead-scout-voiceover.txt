This is the AI Lead Scout dashboard.

For day to day use, start with Qualified and Ready. These are the leads worth checking first.

Click Qualified.

This opens the working queue.

Pick a lead and check the basics. Look at the company name, the score, and the contact details.

If you want to check the premises, click Maps, or Street View.

If you contact the business, click Mark Contacted.

If a meeting is arranged, click Meeting Booked.

If the lead is not a fit, click Not Relevant. That removes it from the working queue.

If that was a mistake, open Not Relevant, then click Add back to queue.

That is the routine. Check Qualified. Contact the good leads. Remove the poor fits. Keep the queue clean.
